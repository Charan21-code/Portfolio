# Design System: Warm Editorial Tech

## Color Palette
*   **Primary Canvas (Background):** `Rich Cream / #FDFBF7`
*   **Surface Cards:** `Soft Sand / #FAF6F0`
*   **Primary Typography:** `Deep Espresso / #1E1916`
*   **Energetic Accent (Primary CTA/Links):** `Terracotta / #E05A36`
*   **Secondary Accent (Tags):** `Warm Gold / #E6A13B`
*   **Grid Lines & Borders:** `Crisp Oat / #E6DFD5`

## Typography Structure
*   **Headings (Display):** Clash Display or Playfair Display. Bold, massive, authoritative.
*   **Body Text (Sans-serif):** Plus Jakarta Sans or Inter. Clean and legible.
*   **Technical Tags:** JetBrains Mono or Fira Code (used strictly for tags like `[ BACKEND SCALE ]`).

## Visual Effects & Animations
*   **Strict Rule:** No neon glows, 3D floating elements, or typing animations.
*   **Hover States:** Text links slant (italicize) or feature a Teracotta underline sweeping from the left.
*   **Project Grid:** On hover, card backgrounds shift from Soft Sand to Rich Cream, and borders highlight slightly in Warm Gold.
*   **Scrolling:** Elements gently fade upward (max 10px translation) as they enter the viewport.
